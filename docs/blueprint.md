# **App Name**: NiftyPulse

## Core Features:

- Market Overview Dashboard: Displays real-time performance indicators and key statistics for NIFTY-FIFTY, including top performers and overall market trends, similar to the initial overview section of the reference image.
- Expert Trader Leaderboard: Showcases top-performing traders with detailed profiles, including their trading history, returns, and number of followers, specifically tailored for NIFTY-FIFTY strategies.
- NIFTY-FIFTY Instrument Details: Provides current buy/sell prices and basic information for individual NIFTY-FIFTY instruments and derivatives, allowing users to see granular data.
- Copy Trading Configuration: Enables users to select and automatically copy trades from chosen expert traders, with customizable risk management settings for NIFTY-FIFTY focused strategies.
- User Authentication & Profile: Allows users to create accounts, log in securely, and manage their personal profile and linked trading accounts.
- AI Market Insights Tool: A generative AI tool that provides data-driven NIFTY-FIFTY market predictions and sentiment analysis, helping users assess trading opportunities and inform their copy trading decisions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, trustworthy blue (#1E4BB3) to represent reliability and professionalism in finance. This color is vibrant enough to stand out against a light background.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue-gray (#F0F3F9) providing a clean and subtle backdrop, ensuring readability and minimizing eye strain. It harmonizes with the primary color.
- Accent color: A bright, clear sky blue (#17E2FF) for call-to-action buttons, key data highlights, and interactive elements. Its high saturation and brightness ensure strong visual contrast and immediate user attention.
- Headline and Body text font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, chosen for its modern, clean, and objective aesthetic, ensuring high readability for financial data and analytical content across the application.
- Clean, modern line icons with occasional subtle fills, consistent with a professional financial technology platform, enhancing clarity without clutter. Emphasize clarity for trading signals and market instruments.
- Grid-based and spacious layout, emphasizing hierarchy and easy navigation. Cards and modular sections for presenting data, statistics, and trader profiles. Ample whitespace to avoid visual clutter and focus on key information, similar to the reference design.
- Subtle, smooth animations for data updates on dashboards, transitions between market instruments, and interactive charts, enhancing user experience without distracting from critical financial information. Keep animations quick and purposeful.
